About Us

Welcome to planMyTrip ! We're a Adventure booking platform that aims to make your adventure experience easy and stress-free. Here's a bit about our goals, mission, and what makes us unique:

  • History
  • Values & Beliefs
  • Founder

How we started: Our founder, Usama, was frustrated with the process of finding stay and booking adventures for his pleasure. He saw an opportunity to create a platform that would make it easier for travelers to find stay and book adventure, and thus planMyDay App was born.

Our mission: To provide a seamless and stress-free Adventure booking experience for travelers.

What makes us unique: Our platform is designed with the traveler in mind. We've created a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to book adventures, and we offer a wide selection of adventures to choose from.

Why customers should choose us: We're committed to providing the best possible Adventure booking experience for our customers. Our platform is easy to use, we offer a wide selection of Adventures to choose from, and we provide top-notch customer service.

Our values: We believe in providing a high-quality service that is easy to use and provides value to our customers. We also believe in being transparent and honest in our business practices.

Our beliefs: We believe that travel is an important part of life, and that it should be accessible to everyone. We also believe that technology can make travel easier and more enjoyable.

About our founder: Usama has over 10 years of experience in the travel industry. He has worked for some of the largest Travel chains in the world, and has a deep understanding of what travelers need and want when it comes to Adventure booking.

Usama started planMyDay App in 2018 with the goal of creating a platform that would make the Adventure booking process easier and more enjoyable for travelers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to common questions we get from our customers:

  • Q: How do I book for an Adventure through your platform? A: Simply search for the city or location you want to have fun in, select your check-in and check-out dates, and choose from the available options. Once you've found the adventure you want to book, simply follow the prompts to complete your booking.
  • Q: Do you offer customer support? A: Yes! Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions or issues you may have.
  • Q: How do I cancel a booking? A: If you need to cancel a booking, simply log in to your account and follow the prompts to cancel your booking. Please note that cancellation policies may vary depending on the adventure and the rate you've booked.
